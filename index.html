Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve in Karnataka?

+

We serve all districts across Karnataka. Whether you're in Bengaluru, Mysuru, Mangaluru, Hubballi, or any other district, our team is ready to assist with your construction project.

How long does a typical residential construction project take?

+

The timeline depends on the size and complexity of the project. A typical 2BHK house takes 8-10 months, while larger projects like villas or apartments may take 12-18 months. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase.

Do you provide structural warranty for your constructions?

+

Yes, we provide a 10-year structural warranty on all our constructions. This covers any structural defects, giving you long-term assurance of the quality and durability of your building.

What is your payment schedule?

+

We follow a milestone-based payment system linked to project completion stages. Payments are only requested after you verify the completion of each construction phase, ensuring transparency and accountability.

Can you help with getting necessary permits and approvals?

+

Yes, we provide complete assistance with all necessary permits, approvals, and documentation required for construction projects. Our team is experienced in dealing with local municipal authorities across Karnataka.
